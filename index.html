What to Look for in a Wholesale Snack Distributor

When it comes to sourcing snacks for your store, not all distributors are created equal. A strong wholesale snack distributor should do more than just supply products—they should support your business goals with quality, selection, and service. Here’s what we believe every business should consider:

1. Product Variety and Inventory

At Pitco Foods, we understand that your customers expect choices—from grab-and-go chips to trending better-for-you snacks. That’s why our product lineup includes thousands of snack items in all categories:

  • Chips, pretzels, popcorn, and nuts
  • Healthy snack options like protein bars and fruit snacks
  • Ethnic and specialty snacks for diverse communities
  • Seasonal or limited-time items to help you stay current

With four warehouse-style locations in San Jose, Oakland, Sacramento, and Hayward, we maintain deep inventories to support a wide range of businesses, from small independent grocers to large-format stores.

2. Pricing and Value

Our commitment is to provide competitive pricing so that our customers can succeed. We believe wholesale pricing should make sense for both sides. That’s why we offer bulk discounts, weekly promotions, and exclusive pricing for members of our Pitco Foods buying program.
We’ve seen firsthand how margins make or break a store. Our mission is to help you stock quality products at prices that leave room for real profit.

3. Reliable Delivery and Access

Convenience matters. Whether you shop in person at one of our four warehouse stores or place orders for scheduled delivery, we make sure your product arrives when you need it. Our team works hard to ensure accuracy and prompt fulfillment every step of the way.
We also offer in-store shopping 7 days a week for customers who prefer to choose their inventory on the spot. You’ll find our facilities clean, organized, and staffed with experienced team members ready to assist.
